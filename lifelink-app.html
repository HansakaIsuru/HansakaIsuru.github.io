LifeLink Android App

A collaborative project focused on improving medication access for differently-abled individuals at home. LifeLink includes three connected mobile apps and a website to ensure seamless prescription management.

Show More Details

Objectives:

  • Enable home-based medication access for differently-abled users
  • Ensure a seamless link between patients, caregivers, and pharmacies
  • Develop a scalable and user-friendly mobile experience

Tools: Android Studio, Flutter, Firebase

Outcome: Successfully built MVP with integrated apps and website, optimized for accessibility
